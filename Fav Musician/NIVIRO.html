NIVIRO Tribute Page!

Bio

Tim Peeters, widely known as NIVIRO, is a Belgian based DJ and EDM music producer born in 1997. After growing up with piano and eurobeat, Tim started producing and performing his own music at the age of 15.
He created NIVIRO in 2016 and his career took after in 2017 after signing several tracks with British Record Label, NCS. He currently has over 800k monthly spotify listeners and is regarded as Belgium's biggest EDM export.
His hit "The Ghost" is currently his most popular song with 22 million views on both Youtube and Spotify.
